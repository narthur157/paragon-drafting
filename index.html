How To

Fill out the below form completely and press "Start Drafting". You will then be taken to a page with three links. Give your team's link to your team-mates and give the opponent's link to your opponents. If there are any spectators, give them the spectator link. Spectators will be unable to participate in the draft, they can only watch it progress. If team links are made public (via accidentally showing links on stream for example) anyone can join and participate in the draft. We recommend viewing the draft lobby in fullscreen mode (F11 shortcut) if you are on a team and streaming a drafting lobby.
